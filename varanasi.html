Varanasi

The Spiritual Heart of India

About Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the world’s oldest continuously inhabited cities, is the spiritual capital of India. Situated on the banks of the Ganges River in Uttar Pradesh, it is known for its sacred ghats, temples, and rich cultural heritage. Pilgrims come from all over the world to take a holy dip in the Ganges.

Key Attractions

  • Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Manikarnika Ghat
  • Evening Ganga Aarti

Best Time to Visit

November to February for pleasant weather and vibrant festivals.

How to Reach

Varanasi is well connected by air, rail, and road. Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport serves domestic and some international flights.
