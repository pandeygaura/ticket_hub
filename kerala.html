Kerala - God's Own Country

About Kerala

Kerala, located on the tropical Malabar Coast of India, is famous for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, tea plantations, and vibrant culture. Known as "God’s Own Country," Kerala offers unique experiences like houseboat cruises, Kathakali dance performances, Ayurvedic treatments, and mouthwatering cuisine.

Top Attractions

  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Munnar Tea Gardens
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Kovalam and Varkala Beaches
  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Best Time to Visit

September to March is the best time to visit Kerala for pleasant weather and to enjoy its scenic beauty.
