Taj Mahal

Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India

About

The Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World, is a magnificent mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Constructed between 1631 and 1648 in Agra, it is renowned for its stunning white marble architecture and intricate craftsmanship.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit the Taj Mahal, when the weather is pleasant and perfect for sightseeing.

Visiting Hours

The Taj Mahal is open from sunrise to sunset every day except Fridays, when it is closed for prayers.

Entry Fee

  • Indian Citizens: ₹50
  • Foreign Tourists: ₹1100
  • Children below 15 years: Free

How to Reach

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra and can be reached via rail, road, or air. The nearest airport is in Agra, about 7 km from the monument.
