About Sikkim

Sikkim, located in the northeastern part of India, is a paradise for nature lovers and spiritual seekers. Known for its snow-capped mountains, colorful monasteries, and vibrant culture, it offers a peaceful escape from the hustle of city life. The mighty Kanchenjunga, the third highest mountain in the world, stands proudly here.

Top Attractions

  • Tsongmo Lake
  • Rumtek Monastery
  • Yumthang Valley
  • Kanchenjunga National Park
  • Nathula Pass

Best Time to Visit

March to June and September to December are ideal to explore Sikkim's beauty.
