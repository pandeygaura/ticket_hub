Majestic Ladakh

The Land of High Passes

About Ladakh

Ladakh, located in the northernmost part of India, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, crystal-clear lakes, and adventurous mountain passes. From Pangong Lake's shimmering blue waters to Nubra Valley's sand dunes, Ladakh is a paradise for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

Popular Attractions

  • Pangong Lake
  • Magnetic Hill
  • Nubra Valley
  • Thiksey Monastery
  • Khardung La Pass

Best Time to Visit

May to September, when the weather is pleasant and most passes are open.

How to Reach

You can reach Ladakh by air via Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport in Leh, or take adventurous road trips via the Srinagar-Leh Highway or Manali-Leh Highway.
