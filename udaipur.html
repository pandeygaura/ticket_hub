Udaipur - The City of Lakes

About Udaipur

Udaipur, located in Rajasthan, is often called the "Venice of the East" and is famous for its stunning lakes, royal palaces, and rich culture. Surrounded by the Aravalli Hills, the city offers a picturesque and romantic charm that attracts travelers from around the globe.

Main Attractions

  • City Palace: A magnificent palace complex with a stunning view of Lake Pichola.
  • Lake Pichola: Famous for boat rides with views of the Lake Palace and surrounding hills.
  • Jag Mandir: An island palace known for its beautiful architecture and gardens.
  • Saheliyon Ki Bari: A historic garden with fountains, kiosks, and marble elephants.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best season to visit Udaipur as the weather is pleasant and perfect for sightseeing.
