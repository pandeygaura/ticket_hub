About Flower Valley

Nestled in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, the Valley of Flowers is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its vibrant meadows of endemic alpine flowers and diverse fauna. The breathtaking scenery, snow-clad peaks, and rich biodiversity make it one of India's most enchanting trekking destinations.

Best Time to Visit

The valley is in full bloom from July to September. During this period, the snow melts, and thousands of colorful flowers paint the valley.

How to Reach

  • Nearest Airport: Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun
  • Nearest Railway Station: Rishikesh / Haridwar
  • Trek Start Point: Govindghat (reachable by road)
